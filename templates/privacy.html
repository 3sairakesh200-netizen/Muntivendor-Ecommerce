Lumio Policy

Privacy Policy

Lumio respects your privacy and keeps your shopping experience simple, secure, and transparent.

Information We Use

We may use your name, email, phone number, delivery address, order history, wishlist activity, and support messages to operate your account and process marketplace orders.

How It Helps

Your information helps us show relevant products, manage checkout, estimate delivery, notify you about orders, prevent abuse, and improve customer support.

Seller And Delivery Sharing

Order details may be shared with the seller and delivery partners only when needed to fulfill, update, return, or support your purchase.

Your Choices

You can update your profile, manage saved addresses, remove wishlist items, and contact Lumio support for account or privacy-related requests.
